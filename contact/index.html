Helmut G. Katzgraber  

Bio

Helmut Katzgraber was born in Lima, Peru and is of Austrian descent. After completing military duties in Austria, he studied physics at ETH Zurich. In 2001 he received his PhD in Physics at the University of California Santa Cruz. After postdocs at the University of California Davis and ETH Zurich, in 2007 he was awarded a Swiss National Science Foundation professorship. In 2009 he joined Texas A&M University, receiving an NSF CAREER award in 2011. Between 2015 and 2020 he held the rank of professor before moving to industry. After two years as principal research manager at Microsoft, he joined Amazon Web Services in 2020 as global practice lead of the Amazon Advanced Solutions Lab. In 2021 he was elected Fellow of the American Physical Society. Since 2025 he is general partner and Chief Science Officer at 55 North, a VC firm focused on advanced compute and quantum technologies. In his spare time he likes to scuba dive.
